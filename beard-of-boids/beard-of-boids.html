BEARD OF BOIDS

A rather determined flock of beard hairs

When: Oct 2018

Who I worked with: Done under Prof Golan Levin .

What I learned: How to create a digital experience that interacts with and responds to the human body while trying to create actual physical feeling in the real world. Done using Face OSC and Processing.

What this was: Beard hairs which followed the viewer's face around. Beard hair is often associated with manliness, but it can also be playful, gross and weird. This project attempts to subvert the meanings inherent in beards to create a playful, determined flock of beard hairs that follow the user's face around.

Feedback received: "This is weird and wonderful" - fellow students.
