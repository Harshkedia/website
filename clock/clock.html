Linguistic Clock

A clock that "tells" you the time

Done under Prof Golan Levin.

Here I made a clock that's a linguistic interpretation of time, with a twist. The idea is to tell time in an interesting way, so 8:41 should become "Ten past Half past Eight", both increasing ambiguity in the understanding of time, as well as being weirdly specific about what the time is. The complexity of the wording increases as time passes, making the length of the words themselves a representation of how much time has passed.

Coming soon: A physical, arduino-based clock that speaks to you.
